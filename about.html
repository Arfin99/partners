About us

Club Swan is a member-based organisation which provides concierge services and related financial services to its members.

Club Swan Partners is a division of Club Swan which provides B2B sales channel for Club Swan's online banking services to businesses who want to offer our membership program to their customers using their own brand and through their own platform..

Funds may be processed and services carried out by one or many of the companies in the Club Swan Group including AU Card Inc, Nvayo Limited and CoinX and related entities.

Membership, concierge and lifestyle services are offered through AU Card, LLC.
